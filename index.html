About Me.

I’m Zeeshan — a Senior Unity Engineer focused on building scalable, production-ready game systems. I specialize in multiplayer architecture, mobile optimization, and clean, maintainable code that performs under real-world conditions. Over the years, I’ve worked on projects ranging from fast-paced mobile titles to networked multiplayer experiences, contributing not just as a developer, but as a systems thinker. I enjoy solving complex technical challenges — whether it’s synchronization, performance bottlenecks, or designing architecture that supports long-term growth.

Beyond implementation, I value structure, clarity, and collaboration. I’ve worked closely with cross-functional teams, contributed to architectural decisions, and helped improve development workflows to ensure projects ship smoothly and sustainably.
